# Strategy & Risk Analysis (SWOT)

A strategic breakdown of The Foundry’s highly debated structural mechanics.

### 📊 SWOT Framework
* *Strengths:* Unrivaled distribution power and massive personal brand equity of the mentors. Up to ₹4 crore in capital injection.
* *Weaknesses & Strategic Risks:* Extremely tight intellectual property (IP) lock-ins and the controversial liability structure (where exits can incur steep structural program fees for applicants).
* *Opportunities:* Redefining the Indian venture capitalist (VC) landscape by creating an institutional "co-founder factory."
* *Threats:* Negative public relations or legal pushback from ecosystem operators regarding strict contract terms.
